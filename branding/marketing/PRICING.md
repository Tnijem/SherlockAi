# Sherlock Law AI - Pricing Model

**Simple, value-based tiers by storage (GB) + users. Annual discount: 20%. Free 30-day trial (10GB/1 user).**

| Tier | Monthly | Storage | Users | Features | Best For |
|------|---------|---------|-------|----------|----------|
| **Starter Mini** | $99 | 10 GB | 1 | Core RAG search, basic analytics, 1 Mac Mini | Solo practitioners |
| **Pro** | $299 | 100 GB | 5 | + Case patterns, drafting aids, team sharing | Small firms (2-10 attys) |
| **Business** | $599 | 500 GB | 10 | + Custom workflows, API access, priority support | Medium firms (10-30 attys) |
| **Enterprise** | $999+ | Unlimited | 20+ | + White-glove setup, unlimited nodes, custom models | Growing firms |

**Add-ons:**
- Extra 100GB: $49/mo
- Extra user: $29/mo
- Mac Mini hardware bundle: $1,499 (one-time)

**Why GB/Users?** Ties to real costs (storage/compute). Transparent vs Harvey's opaque enterprise quotes.

**Billing:** Stripe. No contracts. Pause anytime.
