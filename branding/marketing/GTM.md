# Go-To-Market Strategy: Sherlock Law AI

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Small-medium law firms (1-50 attorneys)
  - Practices: Family law, criminal defense, personal injury, real estate, general practice.
  - Pain points: Data privacy fears (HIPAA, client confidentiality), high cloud costs, BigLaw tools too complex/expensive.
  - Buyer: Managing partners, IT-savvy admins, solo practitioners.
- **Size:** 50K+ US firms under 50 attorneys (ABA stats).

## Positioning
- **Core Message:** "Offline AI for law firms: Harvey's power, without the cloud risk or BigLaw price tag."
- **Differentiators vs Harvey (Cloud):**
  | Feature | Sherlock (Local) | Harvey (Cloud) |
  |---------|------------------|----------------|
  | **Deployment** | Air-gapped Mac Mini | Internet-required |
  | **Privacy** | 100% local, no data leaves | Enterprise cloud (trust OpenAI?) |
  | **Cost** | $99/mo starter | $1000s/user/yr (enterprise) |
  | **Target** | SMB firms | AmLaw 100+ |
  | **Setup** | Plug & play | Custom integrations |
  | **Scalability** | GB/user tiers | Seat-based subs |

## Channels & Tactics
1. **Conferences (High-Intent):**
   - State bar associations, LegalTech expos (e.g., ABA TECHSHOW, ClioCon).
   - Booth demos: Live air-gapped analysis. Swag: "Keep Secrets Local" stickers.
   - Goal: 50 leads/event.

2. **LinkedIn (Inbound/Outbound):**
   - **Content:** Weekly posts: "Why Cloud AI Fails SMB Law Firms" (vs Harvey case studies), privacy horror stories, Sherlock demos.
   - **Ads:** Target "law firm owner" + "legal tech" keywords. $5K/mo budget.
   - **Outbound:** 100 msgs/week to managing partners. Tool: LinkedIn Sales Nav.
   - Influencers: Legal tech podcasters (e.g., LawNext).

3. **Partners (Scale):**
   - IT resellers like NIJEMTech: 20-30% rev share.
   - Bar associations, legal software bundles (Clio, PracticePanther integrations).
   - MSPs for law firms: Co-marketing webinars.

## Timeline & Metrics
- **Q1:** Content engine + LinkedIn launch. 200 leads.
- **Q2:** Conf season + partner onboarding. 1K leads, 10% conversion.
- **Pricing Flywheel:** Free trial (30 days), upsell tiers.
- **CAC Target:** <$500. LTV: $10K+ (2yr avg).

**North Star:** 100 paying firms in Year 1.
